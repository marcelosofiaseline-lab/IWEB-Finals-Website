Basic Information

Character

Draculaura, the adopted daughter of Count Dracula and a student at Monster High, is a sweet, energetic vampire known for her pink-and-black gothic style, friendly and caring nature, and her role as a loyal friend who helps others feel accepted.

Abilities

A vegan vampire with classic traits like no reflection and garlic allergy, Draculaura can levitate, transform into a bat, and communicate with bats, though these powers are still developing. Talented in writing, acting, embroidery, music, and cheerleading, she balances her strengths with vulnerabilities to sunlight, fainting at the sight of blood, and stress-induced pink polka dots.

Student Files

A bright, cheerful student with a friendly and talkative nature. Especially strong in creative subjects like Creative Frighting and Drama, her imagination and performance skills shine. Despite occasional struggles with classroom etiquette, academic performance remains solid in both group and solo work. Known for strong social skills, Draculaura naturally brings friends together and fosters unity within her group.
